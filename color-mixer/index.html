Description

Color Mixer is one of many browser tools available on our worldtools website. It allows users to blend two or more colors in different quantities and see the result color after blending as well as the proportions and colors used to create it.

You will have already a good selection of selected colors situated at the bottom left corner of the mixed color and you can add new color by clicking the button. When you hover a color, and buttons will appear that you can use to add and remove quantities of the hovered color and if you want, you can remove it by clicking the button.
